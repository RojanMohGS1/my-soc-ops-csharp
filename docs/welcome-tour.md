# Welcome Tour — SocOps Blazor App

Welcome! This small Blazor workshop app demonstrates a Bingo-style quiz and multi-agent workshop.

Quick start:

- Run: `dotnet run --project SocOps/SocOps.csproj`
- Open: http://localhost:5166

Key places in the repo:

- Pages and components: SocOps/Pages and SocOps/Components
- Services: SocOps/Services (game logic)
- Styles: SocOps/wwwroot/css/app.css

Try it:

1. Open the app in your browser (local URL above).
2. Click Start to begin a Bingo game and play through the quiz.
3. Explore the `workshop/` folder for guided exercises.
